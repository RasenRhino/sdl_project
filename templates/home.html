About

Our project aims to provide a system for collecting toll tax, which is easy to interact with and hassle free when compared to the current system. It is a new take on the current fastag system deployed on Indian highways. The changes we made are to make the whole process much simpler and making the process uniform by taking out the third party. Currently, various banks and payment wallets sell fastags. Different vehicle owners have fastags from various companies so they get a different service or even different benefits. By this system, we can have a single portal for every vehicle and then link it to a payment method by which the tax will reach the government directly. This makes the process a bit more transparent and removes the additional charges the bank takes for putting the fastag on our vehicles. This system does not require any sticker or chip to be put on our vehicles; it scans the number plate to have a unique id. This system requires having cameras on the entries/exits of a highway, which scan for number plates. After scanning, the total tax to be paid for a journey is calculated and reflected on the website. It also offers officials to check which vehicles passed on a particular entry/exit between a certain periods. They can check the details of these vehicles as well. Using this system, we feel the process of tax collection we become easier for the government and also it will be much easier for people to pay taxes.
